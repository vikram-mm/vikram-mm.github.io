Purpose

This is an online learning portal which allows the students of NITK, Surathkal to adapt modern learn practices. This enables the faculty to set assignments and the students to view and submit assignments. This portal also aids the faculty in their research, by providing details of upcoming conferences and links to latest research papers. Students can also view course details and recommended videos for a better learning experience.
